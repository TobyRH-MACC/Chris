Chris Godber - A Tribute

About Chris

Chris Godber was a passionate and dedicated lecturer in computing and media. He was known for his engaging teaching style, his deep knowledge of the subjects, and his genuine care for his students. He left a lasting impact on the lives of many students who had the privilege of learning from him.

His Legacy

Chris's legacy lives on through the countless students he inspired and the valuable contributions he made to the field of computing and media. His passion for teaching and learning continues to inspire others to pursue their dreams in these fields.
